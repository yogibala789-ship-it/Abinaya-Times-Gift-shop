Privacy Policy

How we protect and manage your personal data

Welcome to Abinaya Times & Gift Shop. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us at abinayatimes@gmail.com.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products, when you participate in activities on our website or otherwise when you contact us.

  • Contact Data: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Order Details: Information related to the customized gifts you order, including photos or text for personalization.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect or receive for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate account creation and logon process.
  • To fulfill and manage your orders. To process payments, returns, and exchanges.
  • To deliver products. To ship the items you've purchased to your address.
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and offer support.

3. Sharing Your Information

We only share information with your consent, to comply with laws, to provide you with services, to protect your rights, or to fulfill business obligations. This might include third-party service providers like delivery partners.

4. Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, please also remember that we cannot guarantee that the internet itself is 100% secure.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You may review, change, or terminate your information at any time. If you have any requests regarding your data, please reach out to us via our contact information.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Revised" date and the updated version will be effective as soon as it is accessible.
